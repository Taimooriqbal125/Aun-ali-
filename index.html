Frequently Asked Questions
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the item is in original condition with all tags attached. Please contact our customer service team for assistance.
How can I track my order?
Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number on our courier partner's website to check your order status.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Please check our shipping page for a complete list of countries and estimated delivery times.
Can I change my order after placing it?
We can make changes to your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, the order may be processed, and we may not be able to make changes. Please contact customer support as soon as possible.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and other major payment methods. You can view all available options at checkout.
